Infrastructure dependence: Quantum‑secure channels (QKD) and reliable IoT sensors require substantial physical deployment, especially in conflict zones where connectivity is fragile.
Recommendations
Robust validation pipeline for H‑VAR: cross‑check with on‑the‑ground surveys and independent NGOs before feeding the metric into the AI.
Open‑source audit layer: publish the symbolic‑logic rules and proof‑checking code so third parties can verify that the “Final Principle” is enforced as intended.
Hybrid delivery: allow fallback to conventional encrypted channels when QKD links are unavailable, with the same zero‑trust checks to maintain security.
